MediScribe User Manual

Professional AI Medical Transcription for Healthcare Professionals

1. Overview

MediScribe is a high-performance, 100% offline transcription tool designed specifically for doctors. It recognizes medical terminology accurately and allows you to type directly into any application, such as Microsoft Word or your EMR system.

2. Voice Recording Mode (Real-Time Dictation)

Transcribe your voice directly into text as you speak.

Start Recording: Click the Microphone icon or press Ctrl + Shift + R.
Dictate: Speak naturally. The AI will process your voice in three stages to ensure accuracy without "hallucinations."
Stop Recording: Click the Stop button or press Ctrl + Shift + R again.
Pro Tip: Ensure the application you want to type into (like Word) is selected and focused before you start dictating.

3. Offline File Mode (Audio Uploads)

Transcribe pre-recorded files from your mobile phone or dictation device.

Navigate to the Offline Files tab.
Drag and drop your audio files (MP3, WAV, M4A) or click Upload Audio.
Wait for the progress bar to complete.
Review the results with timestamps and click Copy to Clipboard.

4. Keyword Expansion Mode

Create shortcuts for templates and long phrases.

Go to Keyword Library and click Add New Keyword.
Enter a shortcut (e.g., "sob") and the expansion (e.g., "shortness of breath").
Enable the Keyword Listener in settings.
Type the shortcut in any app followed by a space to automatically expand it.

5. Google Drive Cloud Sync

Keep your keywords and medical dictionary synchronized across all your computers.

Sync Options:

Auto-Sync: Automatically merges your local data with the cloud every 10 minutes. (Recommended for most users).
Push to Cloud: Manually sends your current computer's library to the cloud, replacing the cloud version.
Pull from Cloud: Downloads the current cloud library to your computer, replacing your local version.
Note: There is a 2-device limit per account. To add a new computer, you must log out from one of your old ones first.

6. AI Personalization & Dictionary

Teach the AI your specific specialty terms.

If the AI misspells a drug or procedure, go to Dictionary Manager.
Add the correctly spelled word to your list.
The AI will now prioritize this spelling every time you dictate.

7. Essential Shortcuts

Ctrl + Shift + M : Show/Hide MediScribe window.
Ctrl + Shift + R : Start/Stop Voice Recording.
Ctrl + Shift + K : Open Keyword Library.

8. Privacy & Security

MediScribe is built with a Security-First architecture:

100% Offline: Your voice and medical dictations are processed locally on your hardware.
No Data Sharing: Your PHI (Protected Health Information) is never sent to any server.
HIPAA Compliant: Meets all standard medical privacy requirements by design.
